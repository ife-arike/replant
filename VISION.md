# Vision

Replant exists to help churches connect as one body across locations.

The vision is to create a secure communication network where churches can share prayer requests, urgent needs, resource opportunities, and persecuted church updates in a structured and trustworthy way.

Replant is not intended to become a ministry promotion platform. It is intended to serve as a connection layer between churches.

## Why This Matters

Churches often serve in isolation even when nearby or aligned in mission. Many needs are known only locally, and many opportunities for support are missed because there is no clear communication channel across churches.

Replant seeks to help close that gap.

## Long-Term Hope

The long-term hope is to build a trusted network where churches can:

- Pray with greater awareness
- Respond to needs faster
- Support the persecuted church
- Share resources responsibly
- Build relationships beyond denominational or ministry silos
