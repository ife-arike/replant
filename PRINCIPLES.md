# Principles

Replant will be guided by the following principles.

## Christ-centered unity

The platform should point toward unity in the body of Christ, not competition between ministries.

## Location-based connection

The structure should prioritize churches by location and region rather than personality, brand, or platform influence.

## Safety within the network

Replant should be a safe space, but not an unmoderated space. Harmful, abusive, exploitative, or suspicious activity must have a path for review.

## Privacy with responsibility

Private communication should be protected. At the same time, users should understand that certain activity may trigger internal safety review.

## No manipulation around money

Churches should not feel lured into giving money. Any donation or support model should be transparent, optional, and mission-aligned.

## Persecuted church priority

The persecuted church should have a distinct and protected place within the platform vision.
