# Replant

**The Church, Connected.**

Replant is a ministry network being built to connect verified Christian leaders across location, prayer, support, and shared burden — locally and globally.

This is not a social media platform. No promotions. No noise. No stage. Just the Body of Christ, reconnected.

> *"I will put sinews on you and bring flesh upon you, cover you with skin and put breath in you; and you shall live."*
> — Ezekiel 37:6–7

---

## The Problem

Churches are serving faithfully in their own communities, but there is often no trusted, structured way to communicate across them. Prayer needs, urgent requests, resource gaps, and persecuted church concerns get scattered across group chats, social media, and isolated networks.

Replant is being built as a safer, more intentional communication layer for the Body of Christ.

---

## The Vision

Location-based connection, not platform-based promotion. The biblical pattern of addressing churches by location informs the structure. Churches should be able to see, pray, respond, and support across regions — without the platform becoming centred on personalities or brands.
