Do

Use the logo on dark backgrounds as the primary application.

Maintain clear space equal to the width of the circle stroke on all sides.

Use the logo at minimum 32px for digital and 0.4" for print.

Use the wordmark "Replant" alongside the mark in Cormorant Garamond.

Don't

Don't recolor the logo to anything other than the brand sky blue (#6BB5E8) or white.

Don't stretch, rotate, or distort the mark in any direction.

Don't place the logo on busy photographic backgrounds without a dark overlay.

Don't use the logo smaller than 32px — the root detail will be lost.

Sky Blue — use for Logo, links, CTAs, section labels, accent borders, city names in scroll, pillar titles, scripture citations, button backgrounds.
Near Black — use for All primary page backgrounds. The brand lives in the dark. Light backgrounds are secondary and used sparingly.
Off White — use for All body text, headings, and wordmark on dark backgrounds. Never use pure white — the warmth of #F0EDE6 is intentional.
Muted tones — use for Subtext, descriptions, placeholders, and supporting copy. Always derived from Off White at reduced opacity (45% or lower).

05

Voice & Tone

Reverent

Every word carries the weight of what this network represents. We don't use casual or flippant language. The Church is holy ground.

"The Persecuted Church section is not a category among others. It is guarded, handled with the full weight Scripture assigns to those who suffer for the Name."

Direct

No corporate speak, no fluff. When something matters, we say it plainly. Leaders are busy. Respect their time.

"No promotions. No noise. Just the network."

Unifying

We speak to the whole Body — not a denomination, not a stream, not a tribe. Language should always draw in, never divide.

"One Scripture. Many churches. One Body."

Rooted

Every structural decision traces back to Scripture. Paul's letters. The churches in Revelation. The prayer of Jesus in John 17.

06

Usage Guidelines

Name Always written as "Replant" — capital R, lowercase rest. Never "REPLANT", "replant", or "Re-Plant". The wordmark on materials uses spaced uppercase caps as a display treatment only.
Tagline "The Church, Connected" is the primary tagline. Secondary: "The Church, Sewn Back Together." Use the primary for brevity, secondary for deeper context.
Digital Dark background (#080808) is always the primary digital treatment. Sky blue (#6BB5E8) for all interactive and accent elements. Fonts loaded from Google Fonts: Cormorant Garamond + DM Sans.
Print Business card: 3.5 × 2 inches. Dark background maintained for print. Logo reproduced in sky blue only. Minimum logo size 0.4 inches. Font: Cormorant Garamond for display, DM Sans for detail text.
Web presence projectreplant.org · connect@projectreplant.org
Netlify hosted. All forms should route to the network contact. No third-party ads or promotions on any Replant property.
Scripture standard The Bible is the only source of truth on the platform and in all Replant communications. KJV used as the citation standard on brand materials. No exceptions.
