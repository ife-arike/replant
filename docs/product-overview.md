# Product Overview

Replant is envisioned as a secure communication platform for churches.

## Primary Users

- Church leaders
- Prayer coordinators
- Trusted ministry workers
- Regional church representatives
- Future verified support partners

## Core Workflows

- Submit a prayer request
- View prayer requests by region or category
- Share needs with trusted churches
- Respond to needs with resources or manpower
- Receive updates related to the persecuted church
- Report unsafe or suspicious activity

## Product Goal

The product should make it easier for churches to communicate clearly and safely without turning the platform into social media.
