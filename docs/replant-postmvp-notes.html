Replant — Post-MVP Notes

Features, decisions & details for after launch

01

Onboarding & Account

MVP 2

Underground church name encryption

When a church selects "Underground Church" as their type, their ministry name should never be shown in full anywhere in the app after signup. It should immediately convert to an encrypted-looking display name (e.g. mus****). This applies everywhere their name surfaces in the app.

MVP 2

Church without walls — location handling

Church without walls (online or otherwise) should have location required — they still have a base location. Underground Church is the only type where location is fully disabled, with a popup: "Location not required for your safety."

MVP 2

Anonymous options popup during registration

If a leader clicks "Yes" to persecution or anonymous during registration, a popup should ask: do you want to keep your name anonymous, your ministry anonymous, or both? This selection is stored and respected across the entire app automatically.

Design

30-day countdown timer on account verification

Account shows a live countdown (e.g. "27 days remaining to confirm your church"). If unconfirmed after 30 days, account is deactivated. Timer functionality to be wired in dev phase.

MVP 2

Branch / parish same-name handling

If multiple branches of a church share the same name, that's acceptable — the emphasis is on the Body, not the name. Location is always shown alongside the church name (unless underground) to distinguish between same-name churches.

Posting, Forms & Visibility

MVP 2

Per-post name anonymity toggle

On any form or submission (prayer request, intercession, heartcry, connect message), the user should be able to toggle their personal name on or off for that specific post. However, whatever they selected in account settings for church/ministry visibility is always respected and cannot be overridden per-post.

Design

Testimonies section on Prayer Wall

Already visualized in wireframes. Needs its own submission form — separate from prayer requests and intercession. Users should be able to reference a previous prayer request when submitting a testimony. Green color treatment throughout.

Persecuted Church

Certain verified or spiritually trusted leaders should be given private access to pressing information (underground church locations, urgent persecution details). The structure for this tier needs more discussion — how leaders are verified, what access means, and governance. Phased out of MVP intentionally.

When a church submits a heartcry, someone needs to respond. Who? How fast? What's the protocol? This is a system-level responsibility question, not just a feature. Needs a defined process before this section goes live.

06

FAQ — Prepared Answers

The following Q&As should be written and live in the FAQ section of the hamburger menu. Add more as they come up.

Is there a limit to how many leaders can register per church?

Yes. A maximum of two (2) pastors or leaders may register per church or ministry on the Replant network.

What if two churches have the same name?

That's perfectly fine. The emphasis on Replant is not on church or ministry names but on the Body as a whole. Where church names appear, the location is always shown alongside (unless the church is underground) to distinguish between them.

Can our church have multiple branches or parishes on Replant?

Yes. Each branch or parish should register separately using the full church name plus its specific location or parish name (e.g. "Grace Chapel — Loganville Branch"). Each branch counts toward the two-leader limit for that specific location.

Are chats on Replant private?

Conversations within Replant are protected within the network and are not shared externally. However, within the platform, certain keywords may trigger an internal review to ensure the space remains safe, godly, and purposeful. Users will be notified of this in the community covenant at signup.

Can we send financial support through Replant?

At this time, Replant does not facilitate direct financial transfers between ministries. We are working on a safe and accountable way to enable this in a future update. For now, support may come through prayer, resources, manpower, and advice.

Is Replant open to house churches and underground churches?

Absolutely. House churches, churches without walls, online churches, and underground churches are not only eligible — they are actively encouraged to join. Anonymous registration is available to protect leaders where needed.
