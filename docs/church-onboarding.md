# Church Onboarding

Replant should prioritize trust during onboarding.

## Possible Onboarding Flow

1. Church leader expresses interest
2. Replant team reviews the church or organization
3. Church receives verified access
4. Church designates approved users
5. Users receive access based on role
6. Church can submit prayer requests, needs, and updates

## Information Needed

- Church name
- Location
- Primary contact
- Contact email
- Role of contact person
- Website or public reference
- Reason for joining Replant

## Open Questions

- Who approves churches?
- Can individuals join without church verification?
- Should churches have one admin or multiple admins?
- How should inactive churches be handled?
