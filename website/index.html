The Vision

Churches were never meant to carry their burdens alone.

Right now, leaders in the same city don't always know each other. Prayer needs go unheard. Resources sit in one church when another is in desperate need. The Body is fragmented.

Replant is a secure network — not a social media platform — where verified Christian leaders can find one another, pray together, and extend real support across geography. Anchored in Scripture. Driven by the Spirit.

Prayer

Post and receive intercession globally. Tagged by location, urgency, and need. Not a feed — an altar.

Connection

Find leaders by city. Reach out directly. No noise, no algorithms — just access to the Body.

Support

Share resources, manpower, and ministry needs across churches who are willing and able.

The Word Only

The Bible is the only source of truth on this platform. No exceptions.

Structured Like the Letters of Revelation

Needs, named and addressed.

Prayer requests aren't generic posts. They're categorized the way Jesus addressed the churches He wrote to.

The Persecuted Church

A protected, access-restricted section. For churches facing real danger — treated with full weight and sobriety.

Set Apart

Some of our brothers and sisters are not free to worship openly.

The Persecuted Church section is not a category among others. It is guarded, restricted to verified leaders, and handled with the full weight Scripture assigns to those who suffer for the Name. If you carry these churches in prayer, this section is for you.
